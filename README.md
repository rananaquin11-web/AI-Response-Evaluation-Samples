This repository contains examples of AI evaluation work, including prompt assessment, response scoring, and quality review.

The goal is to demonstrate structured evaluation of AI-generated content based on accuracy, relevance, instruction-following, and overall quality.

📊 Evaluation Criteria

Each response is evaluated based on:

Accuracy – Is the information correct?
Relevance – Does it answer the prompt?
Clarity – Is it easy to understand?
Instruction Following – Did it follow directions exactly?
Overall Quality – Final rating (1–10)
🧪 Sample Evaluations
Example 1

Prompt:
Explain why the sky is blue in one sentence.

AI Response:
The sky appears blue because molecules in the air scatter blue light from the sun more than other colors.

Score: 9/10

Evaluation:
Accurate and concise. Fully answers the question in one sentence as requested. Slightly simplified but appropriate.

Example 2

Prompt:
List three benefits of drinking water.

AI Response:
Water is important for survival.

Score: 3/10

Evaluation:
Fails to follow instructions. Only one vague statement provided instead of three specific benefits.

Example 3

Prompt:
Write a professional email requesting a meeting.

AI Response:
Hey, can we meet sometime?

Score: 2/10

Evaluation:
Tone is not professional. Lacks structure, context, and required details for a proper meeting request.

🎯 Purpose

These examples reflect real-world AI evaluation tasks such as:

Response grading
Prompt validation
Quality control
Identifying weak or incomplete outputs
